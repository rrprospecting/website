Who We Are

A field-driven exploration company built for the full project life cycle.

R&R Prospecting is developing into a full small-mining and mineral-exploration operation. Our focus extends beyond locating valuable ground. We approach projects from early research and target generation through testing, operational planning, responsible production, and reclamation.

01

Grounded in Fieldwork

Decisions are built from mapping, sampling, observation, practical testing, and direct experience on the ground.

02

Research Before Equipment

Geological structure, mineral history, access, land status, and recovery potential are evaluated before major resources are committed.

03

Responsible Development

Safety, documentation, reclamation, land stewardship, and regulatory awareness are treated as core parts of every project.

Our Mission

Build productive mineral projects without losing sight of the land.

Our long-term mission is to identify and responsibly develop valuable mineral resources while building practical knowledge that can be shared with prospectors, landowners, project partners, and future generations.

Capabilities

One operation. Every stage of the project.

R&R Prospecting is being built to serve projects from the first research question through field evaluation, development, production support, and reclamation.

Prospecting & Target Generation

Regional research, drainage selection, structural targeting, mineral-occurrence review, and field-priority planning.

Mineral Exploration

Geological observations, mapping, sampling programs, test grids, trench and auger concepts, and exploration documentation.

Small Mining Operations

Practical planning for hand methods and small equipment, material handling, recovery systems, field logistics, and production workflow.

Mine Planning Support

Start-to-finish operational planning, access considerations, testing phases, equipment needs, sequencing, and decision points.

Consulting & Evaluation

Preliminary claim and project review, research organization, risk identification, sampling strategy, and practical second opinions.

Reclamation Planning

Reclamation concepts developed alongside exploration and mining plans—not treated as an afterthought at the end.

Education & Training

Field guides, prospecting education, geological fundamentals, sampling instruction, and practical exploration systems.

Project Documentation

Field worksheets, sampling logs, maps, photographs, project records, research summaries, and repeatable decision systems.

Future Resource Development

Gold remains a major focus, with room to expand into gemstones, base metals, industrial minerals, and other mineable resources.

Project Method

From the first map to the final restored surface.

1. Discover Research geology, history, structure, access, and mineral potential.
2. Evaluate Map, sample, test, document, compare, and apply clear stop rules.
3. Develop Build a practical operation around proven ground and realistic equipment.
4. Reclaim Restore disturbed ground and complete the project responsibly.
